# Data Sources for Wildfire Risk Analysis

## Wildfire Data
- Source: US Forest Service Fire Program Analysis Fire-Occurrence Database (FPA-FOD)
- Description: Spatial database of wildfire incidents across the United States, 1992–2020, including location, size, and cause
- Access: Downloaded via Kaggle mirror of FPA-FOD dataset
- Original Data: https://www.fs.usda.gov

## Shapefile - States and Provinces
- Source: Natural Earth Data
- Description: Polygon shapefile of U.S. state boundaries
- Access: [Download here](https://www.naturalearthdata.com/downloads/110m-cultural-vectors/110m-admin-1-states-provinces/)

## Shapefile - USA Counties
- Source: ArcGIS Online
- Description: Polygon shapefile of U.S. county boundaries
- Note: Dataset accessed via ArcGIS Online (proprietary platform)
